About In The Nest

In The Nest was created to make interior design education accessible and practical for everyone. We believe that beautiful, functional spaces shouldn't require a design degree or unlimited budget.

Our mission is to break down complex design concepts into plain language, provide actionable advice, and help you create spaces that truly reflect your personal style and needs.

Whether you're furnishing your first apartment or refreshing a long-time home, we're here to guide you with honest, practical information you can trust.
